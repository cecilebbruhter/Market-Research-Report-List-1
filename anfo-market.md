<p><p>ANFO, or Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil, is primarily used in the explosives industry and comes in two main market types: powder type and granular type. The powder type consists of finely powdered ammonium nitrate mixed with fuel oil, ideal for applications requiring quick detonation. In contrast, the granular type features larger, coarser particles, offering better flow and stability, making it suitable for bulk blasting operations. Both types cater to different operational needs in mining, construction, and quarrying.</p></p>
