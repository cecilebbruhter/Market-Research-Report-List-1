<p><p>Battery Information Management Systems (BMS) are crucial for monitoring and managing battery performance in various applications. The market can be categorized into Centralized BMS and Distributed BMS. Centralized BMS consolidates all battery management functions into a single unit, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for small systems. In contrast, Distributed BMS disperses management functions across multiple modules, enhancing scalability and redundancy, making it suitable for larger, complex systems. Both types aim to optimize battery life and ensure safety.</p></p>
