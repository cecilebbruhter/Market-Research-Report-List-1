<p><p>The beverage labeling and decoration market comprises several types, each serving unique applications. Pressure-sensitive labeling involves adhesive labels that stick when pressure is applied, offering flexibility and ease of application. Glue-applied labeling involves applying adhesive to labels before affixing them to containers, ensuring durability. Sleeve labeling uses a heat-shrinkable material that conforms to the bottle shape, providing full coverage. In-mold labeling embeds labels during the manufacturing process, enhancing durability and aesthetics. Other methods may include digital printing and direct printing techniques.</p></p>
