<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded buzzer market comprises two primary types: piezoelectric and electromagnetic. </p><p>Piezoelectric buzzers utilize the piezoelectric effect for sound production, offering compactness, low power consumption, and a wide frequency range, making them ideal for portable devices. </p><p>In contrast, electromagnetic buzzers operate on an electromagnetic principle, producing sound through vibrating a diaphragm in a magnetic field. They typically deliver louder volumes and are used in applications needing intensified audio output. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing user interaction in electronic devices.</p></p>
