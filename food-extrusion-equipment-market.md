<p><strong>The Food Extrusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-screw Extruder</li><li>Twin-screw Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food extrusion equipment market features two primary types: single-screw extruders and twin-screw extruders. Single-screw extruders utilize a single screw for material processing, offering simplicity and lower operational costs, ideal for manufacturing snacks and cereals. In contrast, twin-screw extruders employ two intermeshing screws for enhanced mixing and flexibility, enabling complex formulations and better heat transfer. This makes them suitable for products requiring precise texture and flavor profiles, such as pet food and specialty grains.</p></p>
