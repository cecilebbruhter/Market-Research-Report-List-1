<p><p>The medical fibrin adhesive market is categorized into several types based on their composition and application methods. Water-based adhesives use a liquid formulation that activates upon contact with blood, promoting faster healing. Solvent-based adhesives rely on organic solvents for adhesion, offering strong bonding but may pose safety concerns. Solid-based adhesives are pre-formed, providing reliable attachment in surgical procedures. Hot melt-based adhesives are thermoplastic materials that liquefy when heated and solidify upon cooling, ensuring durable adhesion in various medical applications.</p></p>
