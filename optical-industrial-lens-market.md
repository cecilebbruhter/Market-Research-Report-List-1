<p><p>The optical industrial lens market is categorized into several types based on mounting specifications. C-Mount lenses feature a standard 1-inch diameter thread, offering versatility for various cameras. CS-Mount lenses are similar but have a shorter flange distance, ideal for compact applications. F-Mount lenses are primarily used with Nikon cameras, known for their stability and compatibility. S-Mount lenses, often used in smaller, specialized cameras, focus on compact design. Other lenses include unique mounts catering to specific industrial needs, enhancing application flexibility.</p></p>
