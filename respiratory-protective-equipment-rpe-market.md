<p><p>The Respiratory Protective Equipment (RPE) market consists of various types: Non-Powered Air Respirators (NPAR), which rely on ambient air and include masks and filters; Powered Air Respirators (PAR), which utilize a battery-powered blower to supply filtered air, enhancing comfort and functionality; and Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA), providing a portable, independent air supply for confined or hazardous environments. Each type serves distinct applications, ensuring safety for users exposed to airborne hazards in diverse industries.</p></p>
