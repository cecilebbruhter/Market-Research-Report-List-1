<p><p>The translucent films market comprises several key types, each with unique properties. Polypropylene (PP) is lightweight and has a high tensile strength, ideal for packaging and industrial uses. Polystyrene (PS) is valued for its rigidity and cost-effectiveness, often employed in disposable products. Other materials may include bio-based and specialty films, expanding market versatility.</p></p>
